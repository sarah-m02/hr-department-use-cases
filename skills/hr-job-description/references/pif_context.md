# PIF Glossary — Strict, Sourced

**This file is a GLOSSARY, not a description.** Every term below has been
verified against a page on `pif.gov.sa` or `careers.pif.gov.sa` (via a
`site:` search that surfaced the exact wording, or via a leadership page).
Terms not verified against a PIF source are **not in this file**.

The `hr-job-description` skill treats this glossary as literal:

- If a PIF-specific term is needed and it is not in this glossary → do NOT
  invent one; ask the user via the AskUserQuestion "Other" free-text
  fallback and use whatever the user types verbatim.
- Every entry is annotated with a source.
- Verbatim wording matters — do not rephrase capitalized names or program
  titles, even slightly.

Verification date: 2026-07 (direct WebFetch on `pif.gov.sa` returned 403
in this session; verified via `site:` search snippets from Google, which
return PIF's own page copy).

## 2. Investment portfolios — the CURRENT three-portfolio structure (verified)

Under PIF's 2026-2030 strategy, PIF's investments are organized into **three
portfolios**. This is the current structure — the older six-pool taxonomy
(Saudi Equity Holdings, Saudi Sector Development, etc.) is HISTORICAL and
must NOT be used in a 2026+ JD.

Source: `pif.gov.sa/en/our-investments/investment-portfolios/`

| Portfolio (verbatim) | PIF's own description |
|---|---|
| **Vision Portfolio** | *"Catalyzes development of six ecosystems within the local economy… aims to leverage synergies across strategic sectors, maximize value for PIF portfolio companies, and continue to drive the growth of the local economy."* |
| **Strategic Portfolio** | *"Actively manages key strategic assets, maximizing opportunities, building strategic partnerships, and scaling national champions to connect them with global markets."* |
| **Financial Portfolio** | *"Maximizes risk-adjusted returns through thematic investments, delivering resilient value and building strategic international relationships that expand access to global opportunities."* |

## 3. The six ecosystems under the Vision Portfolio (verbatim)

Source: `pif.gov.sa/en/our-investments/investment-portfolios/`

1. **Tourism, Travel & Entertainment**
2. **Urban Development & Livability**
3. **Advanced Manufacturing & Innovation**
4. **Industrials & Logistics**
5. **Clean Energy, Water & Renewables Infrastructure**
6. **NEOM**

Do NOT reorder, rename, or abbreviate these — they are verbatim from PIF's
site. In particular, do NOT write *"Tourism and Entertainment,"* *"Urban
Development,"* or *"Clean Energy"* as shorthand.

## 6. PIF's organizational hierarchy (five tiers)

Understand this before writing "the role sits in X." The tiers are:

1. **Portfolio** — Vision / Strategic / Financial *(three total, per §2)*
2. **Ecosystem** — one of the six under Vision Portfolio *(per §3)*
3. **Sector** — verified example: *Transport & Logistics Sector*. Likely sits under a portfolio or ecosystem
4. **Division / Department** — the units in §4 and §5
5. **Section / Team** — the sub-units listed above

A JD's Work Details Block should identify the role at the most specific
verified tier available. If the user knows a more specific unit (e.g. a
sub-team) but it's not in this glossary, use the free-text fallback and
quote them verbatim.

## 7. Governance and leadership roles (verified from press releases and leadership page)

- **Chairman of the Board** — do NOT name in a JD body
- **Governor** — do NOT name
- **Deputy Governor** (two roles created in 2021)
- **Chief Operating Officer** — leads Shared Services function; leads digital / technology systems and infrastructure
- **Chief Financial Officer**
- **Chief of Staff** — leads Chief of Staff Division; also serves as Secretary General to the Board; oversees Data and Committees Governance, strategic planning, PMO, and portfolio company affairs
- **Secretary General to the PIF Board of Directors**
- **Head of [Division]** — e.g. *"Head of Local Real Estate Investment Division,"* *"Head of Transport & Logistics Sector"*

Reports-to language in a JD may reference *"Head of [Division]"* or
*"Chief [X] Officer"* by title but must never name individuals.

## 10. What NOT to invent

- Do NOT invent department names beyond what §4 and §5 list. If a role sits
  in a function that isn't in this glossary (e.g. "Internal Audit," "Sustainability
  Office," "IR"), ask the user for the exact team name via the free-text
  fallback and use it verbatim.
- Do NOT reference the historical six-pool taxonomy (Saudi Equity Holdings,
  Saudi Sector Development, Saudi Real Estate and Infrastructure, Saudi
  Giga-Projects-as-a-pool, International Strategic Investments, Diversified
  Global Investments Portfolio, Treasury Portfolio) in any 2026+ JD. If you
  find yourself needing one, you're using an outdated framing.
- Do NOT use the word "pool" — PIF's own word is **"portfolio."**
- Do NOT expand "PPA" — the acronym is not publicly documented; use "PPA
  Department" as-is.
- Do NOT invent a job-level ladder (e.g. Vice President / Senior Vice President
  seniority bands) — use whatever level the user specified via Question D
  and don't infer PIF-internal ladder details.

## 11. Hard-content prohibitions

The JD MUST NOT contain:

- Any specific compensation figure, range, bonus structure, allowance amount,
  or benefit dollar value
- Any Vision 2030 target percentage or year
- The name of any Saudi national program (Saudization, Nitaqat, azm, HRDF, etc.)
- Named individuals (Governor, Chairman, Deputy Governor, division heads)
  in the JD body
- Any specific AUM figure or exact headcount number
- Any mention of Arabic language as required, preferred, or optional
- Any "Recommended" label on an MCQ option
- Any invented department, division, or team name (see §10)
